Welcome to KIDSLAND!

At KIDSLAND, we nurture curious minds and joyful hearts! Established in 2014, we provide a safe, play-based learning environment for children aged 18 months to 11 years, including after-school care and specialized tuition for kids aged 4–11 years. Our mission is to inspire creativity, confidence, and lifelong learning through exploration and discovery.

Age Groups & Offerings

18 months–2 yrs : (Daycare) Gentle routines, sensory play, and a nurturing environment to build trust and early motor skills.

2–3 yrs : (Play School) Social skill-building and creative play with music, art, and storytelling.

3–4 yrs : (Preschool/Nursery) Structured play-based learning

4–6 yrs : (Kindergarten) Foundational academics

Our Play-Based Approach

Learning Through Play: Interactive games, storytelling, and creative arts.

Child-Centered Activities: Tailored to individual interests and developmental stages.

Physical Development: Daily outdoor play, dance, and motor skill exercises.
